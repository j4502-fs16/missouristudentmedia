About

KCOU 88.1 FM is a non-commercial station run entirely by University of Missouri students. The station serves as an outlet for the newest in music from numerous genres, the latest in campus and community news, Mizzou sports, programs devoted to many different forms of music otherwise ignored by most commercial radio stations in the Midwest.
