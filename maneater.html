About The Maneater

The student voice of MU since 1955, The Maneater publishes a weekly newspaper on Wednesdays and daily updates online during the fall and spring academic semesters. The staff is made up entirely of students, editorially independent from the university, School of Journalism, student government or any other campus entity.

JOIN

The Maneater is eager to welcome any new staff member, regardless of previous experience or college major. We offer opportunities including staff writers, photographers and columnists that will enrich your college experience.
